About Me

My name is Kyeongan (Karl) Kwon. I was born in Seoul, Korea, where grew up and studied a bachelor degree in computer science (software enginnering) and live in Houston, TX, where study and research data visualization and HCI topics for a PhD degree. A former software engineer at the TmaxSoft, I have researched and developed enterprise software collaborated with top companies such as SAMSUNG, IBM and HP. Now I am a member of the Computational Physiology Lab. (CPL) at the University of Houston since Jan 2013. I have been working under Prof. Ioannis Pavlidis on Human-computer Interaction and data visualization. Priory to that, I used to work the i2C Lab under Prof. Larry Weidong Shi working on Mobile HCI and mobile security projects.
